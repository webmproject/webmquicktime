Thank you for installing the WebM QuickTime package.

It contains:
  VP8 Encoder
  VP8 Decoder
  WebM Importer
  WebM Exporter
  XiphQT

These components enable your computer to create and play WebM files in QuickTime applications.

XiphQT depends on a number of open-source libraries. See the COPYING.*.txt files in the WebM QuickTime disk image for their corresponding distribution terms.
